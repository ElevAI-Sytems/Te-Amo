Our Story

Under dynamic new ownership.

We're redefining the player experience at The Hayes — a club built around community first, competition second, and good times always.

Whether you've never picked up a racket or you're chasing your next tournament, you'll find your level here. Sunlit courts, a proper café and bar, and a welcome that's as warm as a Mediterranean afternoon. This is padel the way it's meant to be played — together.
